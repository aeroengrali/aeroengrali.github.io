Airbus A320 SolidWorks CAD

Solid model with dimensioned drawings and renders

CAD

An A320-class airliner modelled in SolidWorks, with a full multi-view engineering drawing (dimensioned top/side blueprints) and isometric renders. An independent CAD exercise from public reference dimensions, no manufacturer data.

Source on GitHub

Highlights

  • Parametric solid modelling of the fuselage and wing form
  • Dimensioned three-view engineering blueprint
  • Isometric and in-scene renders of the model
  • Built from public reference dimensions; no Airbus proprietary data
